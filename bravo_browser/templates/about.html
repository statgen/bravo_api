About BRAVO

This version of BRAVO variant browser shows chromosome locations, alleles, functional annotations, and allele frequencies for 705 million variants observed in 132,345 deeply sequenced (>38x) genomes from the TOPMed data freeze 8.

The information is shared by TOPMed studies from Phases 1, 2, and 3, and is available for users that agree to the terms.

The sequencing analysis pipeline consists of two major processes:

  • Harmonization of read alignments form the sequence data provided by the sequencing centers;
  • Joint variant discovery and genotype calling across studies using the GotCloud pipeline.
More detailed description of data processing methods for each data freeze is available on the TOPMed page.

If you have a question about BRAVO, contact bravo-group@umich.edu
